## SLIDE 7 — THE THREE-PART ROI

### Every Prospect Gets This Framework

**1. Vref Hard Asset Value**
- 100% ROI at installation, 90% at year 2
- "The only mod that pays for itself before you fly it"

**2. Soft Value (8 categories, rated 1–5)**

**3. Fuel Savings (real data)**
- Example: CJ2 at 300 hrs/yr → ~$51,800/year savings
- Payback from fuel alone: under 4 years (before counting Vref)

**Speaker notes:** Step 1 wins the rational argument. Step 3 wins the economic argument. But Step 2 — the soft value — is what wins the emotional argument.
