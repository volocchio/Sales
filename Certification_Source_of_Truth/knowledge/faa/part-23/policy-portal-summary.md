# FAA Small Airplane Regulations And Policies Portal Summary

## Why This Portal Matters

The FAA Small Airplane Regulations and Policies portal is the working bridge between Part 23 rule text and day-to-day certification execution. It is where the repo should look for accepted means of compliance, policy explanations, ACs, and other small-airplane implementation material.

## How To Use It

- Use the portal when a Part 23 requirement is clear at a high level but the acceptable substantiation path is still unclear.
- Use it to identify whether the FAA already points to an accepted standard, policy memo, AC, or COS material relevant to the issue.
- Record the specific downstream source in this repo after review rather than treating the portal itself as the final authority.

## Working Interpretation

The portal is a discovery and orientation source, not normally the final citation you want in a compliance report. It should usually lead the team toward the actual AC, policy statement, standard, or COS material that should then be cataloged separately.

## Source Boundary

This summary describes the portal's role in the certification workflow. It does not convert the portal into a stand-alone authority.
