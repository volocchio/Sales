# 14 CFR Part 25 Summary

## Why This Part Matters

Part 25 is the core technical authority for transport-category airplane certification. It is a much broader and more integrated rule set than Part 23, with major emphasis on flight performance, structures, systems safety, EWIS, operating limitations, ICA, and complex failure-condition management.

## Practical Role In This Repo

- Treat Part 25 as the primary technical spine for transport-category programs and transport-category STCs.
- Pair it with Part 21 for changed-product and STC applicability analysis.
- Use it to structure reports around the major technical domains that usually drive FAA and ODA review: flight, structures, systems, equipment, ICA, and operating limitations.

## High-Value Themes

- Flight and performance: proof of compliance, speeds, takeoff and landing performance, climb, control, stability, stalls, and handling.
- Structures: loads, factors of safety, proof of structure, gusts, maneuver envelopes, emergency landing conditions, and damage tolerance.
- Systems safety: Part 25 explicitly integrates failure-condition severity, probability, crew effects, and certification maintenance requirements.
- Continued airworthiness: ICA, airworthiness limitations, EWIS ICA, lightning protection features, and damage-tolerance outputs are built into the certification basis, not bolted on later.
- Operating information: AFM content, limitations, placards, ETOPS, icing limitations, and required performance data are part of the approval package.

## Sections That Commonly Drive Reviews

- Section 25.1 and Section 25.2: applicability and retroactive change triggers.
- Section 25.4: failure-condition definitions that feed systems work.
- Subpart B: flight, performance, control, stability, stalls, and handling.
- Subpart C: structural loads, proof, damage tolerance, emergency landing, and lightning protection.
- Section 25.629: aeroelastic stability.
- Section 25.671, 25.672, and 25.1309: flight controls, automatic systems, and systems safety.
- Section 25.1419 and 25.1420 plus Appendices C and O: icing and SLD compliance paths.
- Section 25.1501 through 25.1587: operating limitations, AFM, and required operating information.
- Section 25.1529 and Appendix H: Instructions for Continued Airworthiness.
- Subpart H and related ICA material: EWIS requirements.
- Appendix K: ETOPS where applicable.

## Working Interpretation

For Part 25 programs, a usable source-of-truth repo has to preserve the cross-links between structural, systems, and operational requirements. A narrowly siloed report set often misses Part 25's real pattern, where the approved design, operating limitations, maintenance program, and failure-condition management all support one another.

## Source Boundary

This summary is an internal normalization of the current eCFR Part 25 text. It is not a substitute for the regulation itself.
