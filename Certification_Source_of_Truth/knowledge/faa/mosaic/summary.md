# MOSAIC Summary

## What It Is

MOSAIC is the FAA rulemaking effort titled `Modernization of Special Airworthiness Certification`.

## Status

- The 2025 Federal Register action is the final rule.
- The 2023 Federal Register action was the NPRM.
- Main effective date: 2025-10-22.
- Some delayed provisions take effect: 2026-07-24.

## Why It Matters To This Repo

MOSAIC is a good example of how this repository should handle a source:

- record authoritative metadata
- distinguish proposal from final rule
- summarize operational meaning
- create reusable internal positions where needed

## Initial Practical Takeaways

- Do not treat NPRM text as the current requirement once the final rule exists.
- Always capture effective dates because certification planning and report language depend on them.
- Distinguish legal authority from explanatory guidance portals.

## Follow-On Work For This Topic

- Extract the major differences between NPRM and final rule into discrete compliance positions.
- Link affected aircraft categories, pilot privileges, and airworthiness pathways.
- Add any internal reporting language you want teams to reuse.
