# Initial FAA Ingestion Backlog

## Objective

This is the first-pass backlog for turning the repo from structure into an actual certification knowledge base.

## Priority 1: Core Regulatory Framework

- 14 CFR Part 21
- 14 CFR Part 23
- 14 CFR Part 25
- 14 CFR Part 27
- 14 CFR Part 43
- 14 CFR Part 45
- 14 CFR Part 91

## Priority 2: FAA Guidance And Policy

- Small Airplane Regulations and Policies portal
- relevant Advisory Circulars for Part 23 and normal-category certification
- FAA Orders that affect certification process and compliance findings
- policy memoranda and issue papers relevant to your aircraft and STC work

## Priority 3: Specialized Topic Buckets

- certification basis development
- means and methods of compliance
- flight test and handling qualities
- structures and loads
- electrical and avionics installations
- continued airworthiness and ICA expectations
- noise and operating limitations

## Priority 4: Reusable Internal Knowledge

- standard compliance positions
- standard report language
- issue resolution patterns
- recurring FAA concerns by topic

## Intake Rule

Do not add a source to the reusable catalog until you know:

- what it is
- whether it is authoritative
- whether it is current
- what projects it affects
- whether it creates a reusable compliance position
