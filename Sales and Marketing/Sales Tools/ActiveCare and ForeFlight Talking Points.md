# ActiveCare Warranty & ForeFlight Integration — Sales Talking Points

## ActiveCare Warranty

### What It Is
ActiveCare is Tamarack's extended warranty program for SMARTWING™ Active Winglet systems. It provides ongoing coverage for the ATLAS load alleviation system and associated components beyond the standard warranty period.

### How to Use It in Sales

**As a closing tool:**
> "Every installation is eligible for our ActiveCare Warranty. It's extended coverage for the SMARTWING™ system — so you're not just getting the performance upgrade, you're getting the peace of mind that comes with it."

**For The Skeptic persona:**
> "I know reliability matters to you. ActiveCare is our commitment that we stand behind every installation. We don't just install and walk away — we support the system for the long term."

**For fleet/charter operators:**
> "ActiveCare means predictable maintenance costs for your SMARTWING™ system. No surprises. That's important when you're running a business on these aircraft."

### Sign-Up
Owners can enroll at tamarackaero.com or through their sales representative at installation.

## ForeFlight Performance Integration

### What It Is
Tamarack has partnered with ForeFlight to deliver **fleet-wide performance data profiles** for all SMARTWING™-equipped aircraft. This means pilots using ForeFlight can access accurate performance calculations that reflect their aircraft's actual SMARTWING™ capabilities — not the stock OEM data.

### Why This Matters

**For pilots:** No more manually adjusting performance numbers. ForeFlight automatically calculates range, fuel burn, climb performance, and descent profiles using SMARTWING™ data.

**For flight planning:** Accurate performance profiles mean:
- Realistic range rings that show the true SMARTWING™ range
- Correct fuel planning that reflects the lower burn rates
- Proper climb gradient calculations for high-altitude airports
- Better go/no-go decisions based on real capability

### How to Use It in Sales

**For tech-savvy owner-pilots (Go-Getter, Old Pro):**
> "When you open ForeFlight after your installation, your performance profiles are already updated. You'll see the real range rings — not the stock numbers. Punch in a trip you've always needed a fuel stop for, and watch the fuel planning show you can make it direct."

**For demo flights:**
> Show the prospect the ForeFlight performance profile during pre-flight planning. Let them see the range ring difference between stock and SMARTWING™ in real time.

**For The Skeptic:**
> "You don't have to take our word for the performance numbers. ForeFlight has independently integrated our data into their platform. Plan a trip in ForeFlight with SMARTWING™ profiles and see for yourself."

### Key Talking Points

1. **ForeFlight is the industry standard** — used by the vast majority of business aviation pilots. Having SMARTWING™ profiles in ForeFlight is mainstream validation.

2. **Fleet-wide data** — these profiles aren't based on theoretical performance. They're built from actual fleet data across 220+ installations.

3. **Seamless experience** — no extra apps, no manual adjustments. It just works inside the tool pilots already use every flight.

4. **Planning confidence** — pilots can accurately plan missions that the stock aircraft can't complete, knowing the numbers are real.
